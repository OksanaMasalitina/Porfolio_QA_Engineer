Our Reasons Why We Love Cats

There are literally hundreds of reasons why we love our cats. Ask any cat lover why they love cats so much and you’ll likely get an earful of reasons. You might even be subjected to tons of cute cat pics and videos. That’s because not only do we love our furry felines but we love to show and tell others how much we love them.





Cuteness

Cats are adorable. They exude cutely. They make us smile and warm our hearts with their playful antics. They play till they’re exhausted then jump into our laps to win our hearts with their bright eyes and soothing purrs.





Intelligent

Cats are known for being intelligent creatures that can learn tricks and behaviors just like dogs. Cats are curious and inquisitive so they're always exploring and getting into unforeseen mischief! Cats are sensitive as well and offer a sense of security since they will alert you if someone is approaching your home.





Great Companions

Cats have their own unique personalities, from frisky and playful to stubborn and lazy. There are cats for everyone’s personal needs. No matter what personality you have there is a cat that will make a great companion for you!





Other Reasons Why Cats Make Great Pets :

  • Independent
  • Low Maintenance
  • Affectionate
  • Entertaining
  • Hunters
  • Clean

    • ...and just because they're beautiful.
